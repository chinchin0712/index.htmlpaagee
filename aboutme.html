About Me, Page 1

What type of person Am I?

Hi! I'm a soft girl with a gentle heart, tahimik, mahiyain, pero sobra akong clingy once I'm comfortable. I'm the type who loves hugs, kisses, and any kind of warm physical touch. Minsan jolly ako, minsan nakakatawa, pero most of the time I'm calm, soft, and quiet.

I'm an overthinker, but I'm also mabait, masayahin, and surprisingly mature kahit playful ako. I have a very long patience, and I always try to understand everyone around me. I may be "mahina sa lahat ng bagay," pero my heart is strong in loving people genuinely.

I'm the type of friend who's?

I'm the type of friend na palaging nandiyan, the listener, the loyal one, the "mother friend," the clingy one, and the super protective friend. When I care about someone, I really care deeply. I'm soft, gentle, and full of affection. I give warm presence, quiet comfort, and silent loyalty.

What I Need

I need affection, understanding, honesty, reassurance, and someone who sees my softness as a strength. I don't ask for much — just warm love and gentle presence.

What I Want in Life

A simple and peaceful life. To be loved genuinely. To be appreciated. To grow and heal slowly.

My Goals & What I Can Achieve

My goal is to become a better version of myself little by little. I want to achieve my dreams step by step, kahit mabagal basta tuloy tuloy. I want to improve my skills, gain confidence, and become stronger emotionally and mentally.

Possible achievements:

  • Improve my cooking skills
  • Finish my studies successfully
  • Build self confidence
  • Learn new skills
  • Help my family
  • Reach my dream career
  • Become financially stable someday
  • Grow as a kind and loving person

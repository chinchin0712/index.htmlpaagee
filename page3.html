Inner Strength & Future Dreams

Finding light in the shadows and courage in my passions

The Weight of Silence

I’ve often struggled with the things that go unnoticed by others but feel incredibly heavy to me. There is a specific kind of pain in wanting to speak but feeling like your words won’t be understood—or worse, that they’ll be judged. It is hard to navigate days when everything feels overwhelming and you just want a safe place to lean on, someone who will listen without fixing, just to let the emotions breathe.

My greatest weakness has always been this silence. It’s the feeling of needing someone to tell me, “I’m proud of you,” but staying quiet because I’m afraid my burdens will be too much for them. I’ve spent so much time pretending I can handle it all, while secretly seeking heartfelt words of support just to feel safe in my own skin.

Where My Strength Begins

Surprisingly, my true strength comes from those very same emotions. I’ve realized that being affected deeply by words is also a gift—it means I have a heart that feels. My strength is renewed whenever I find someone who truly hears me, who sees me at my lowest and chooses to offer understanding instead of judgment.

I’ve learned that I don't have to pretend to be invincible. My courage shows up in the little things—in the way a kind word can lift my spirit and give me the bravery to keep going. I am finding that my strength isn't about never falling; it’s about having the heart to rise again because I know I am understood.

My Dreams: Crafts, Cooking, and Creation

When the world feels too loud, I find my peace in the things I create with my hands. I have a growing passion for the culinary arts—there is something so healing about cooking. It’s more than just following a recipe; it’s about the patience, the flavors, and the joy of sharing something handmade with the people I love. I dream of perfecting my skills in the kitchen, turning simple ingredients into memories.

Alongside cooking, I find myself lost in the world of crafts and sketching. Whether it’s random flowers in my notebook or small DIY projects, crafting allows me to turn my "unsaid thoughts" into something beautiful and tangible. These aren't just hobbies; they are my goals. I want to spend my life creating—whether through food, art, or kindness—building a world where I can express myself fully and without fear.
